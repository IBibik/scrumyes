How to become Scrum Master

Team

You will not become ScMa just by completing certification - what really counts is experience. However, if you do have experience potential employer tries to get some proof of this experience. This is how they try to protect themselves from fraud. Basically, it is very difficult for interviewer if the interviewer is not ScMa to get feeling about potential ScMa for the role he interviews. Since it is relatively easy to read the learning material and answer the questions versus knowing how to act and to be able to deliver. So my feedback will be doing your homework and be self-aware. Certifications I have analyzed basically devided to 3 main groups:

  • 1. Expensive one that offer few days in the classroom and a test. I believe this certificate is more for organizations that want to upskill their ScMa and looking for an easy solution. I personally don’t believe that 2 days in the classroom make any difference however if this training is taken by experienced ScMa it can add value vs. just picking up the theory.
  • 2. Cheap and easy- Cheap test you can execute many times just to get certificate to add to your CV headhunters might not know how easy it is to get such certification.
  • 3. Affordable price but more challenging test – this one should be the golden middle for the employer and for a candidate. To pass, the candidate should know some basics also there is no financial barrier to get it.
Saying all this certificate by itself adds zero value since Scrum is a simple framework to learn but hard to practice. *As of today from available certificates I would recommend https://www.scrum.org/.

See below some examples of Scrum Master Interview questions:

  • * Describe your day as Scrum Master?
  • * What are your main responsibilities?
  • * What value do you are adding to the team?
  • * How do you measure velocity?
  • * How do you deal with conflict?
  • * How you deal with transformation from Waterfall to agile scrum?
  • * How do you handle your PO?
  • * Assume you have to become Scrum Master of the team that never practiced Scrum before, What problems do you predict?
  • * You start project ABC please describe your actions you from day 1 till release?
  • * What are your additional contributions to the team ?
  • * How do you execute and moderate planning meeting?
  • * Identify all of your stakeholders?
  • * What is your role as Scrum Master?
  • * What is the difference between Scrum Master and Project manager?
  • * If customer complains about low quality of deliverables at the end of the Sprints what you will propose to customer in order to solve this problem?
  • * What are the most critical meetings during Sprint execution?
  • * What skills make you great Scrum Master?
