How to become Scrum Master

Experience

You will not become ScMa just by completing certification - what really counts is experience.

However, if you do have experience potential employer tries to get some proof of this experience.

Certification is a tool potential employers use for screening.

Certifications

There 3 main types of certifications on the market:

  • Expensive one that offer few days in the classroom and a test. I believe this certificate is more for organizations that want to upskill their ScMa and looking for an easy solution. I personally don’t believe that 2 days in the classroom make any difference however if this training is taken by experienced ScMa it can add value vs. just picking up the theory.

  • Cheap and easy- Cheap test you can execute many times just to get certificate to add to your CV headhunters might not know how easy it is to get such certification.

  • Affordable price but more challenging test – this one should be the golden middle for the employer and for a candidate. To pass, the candidate should know some basics also there is no financial barrier to get it.

Saying all this certificate by itself adds zero value since Scrum is a simple framework to learn but hard to practice.

*As of today from available certificates I would recommend https://www.scrum.org/.

Scrum Master Interview Questions


  • Describe your day as Scrum Master?

  • What are your main responsibilities?

  • What value do you are adding to the team?

  • How do you measure velocity?

  • How do you deal with conflict?

  • How you deal with transformation from Waterfall to agile scrum?

  • How do you handle your PO?

  • Assume you have to become Scrum Master of the team that never practiced Scrum before, What problems do you predict?

  • You start project ABC please describe your actions you from day 1 till release?

  • What are your additional contributions to the team ?

  • How do you execute and moderate planning meeting?

  • Identify all of your stakeholders?

  • What is your role as Scrum Master?

  • What is the difference between Scrum Master and Project manager?

  • If customer complains about low quality of deliverables at the end of the Sprints what you will propose to customer in order to solve this problem?

  • What are the most critical meetings during Sprint execution?

  • What skills make you great Scrum Master?
